My work journey

Computer geek and a tech enthusiast that never stops learning.

In my previous professional work experience, I've worked as a video production specialist for more than 5 years. Planning, creating and editing videos including motion and sound design. Working on every detail including lighting setup, from concept to completion. In the past I've worked on projects with a lot of clients, locally and globally - whether through my main job or freelance. Also worked on big events like allweb.mk and allweb.al.

Currently I'm working as a Post-Production Specialist. Creating and editing short-form videos, online hybridization courses, 2D Animations, including color grading & sound design, while dedicating my free time to continue educating myself.
Currently I am spending my time in Blender, learning how to create 3D Models! You can check some of my video production work down below!
